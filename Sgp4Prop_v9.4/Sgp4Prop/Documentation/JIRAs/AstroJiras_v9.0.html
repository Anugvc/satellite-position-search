Tom, Zach,

 

Good news-- your M1 build of AFSPC SGP4 is working great on Mac arm64 native SOAP! 😊

 

Quick SOAP benchmark using propagating 23,600 NORAD TLEs:

 

MacBook Pro M1 (arm64-compiled SGP4 gfortran) = *30 fps*

Intel iMac Pro (SGP4 compiled with Intel compiler) = 25 fps

MacBook Pro M1 (Intel-compiled SGP4 on M1 using Rosetta2 emulation) = 20 fps

 

I wasn’t expecting great performance with gfortran-compiled SGP4, but I’m pleasantly surprised.
